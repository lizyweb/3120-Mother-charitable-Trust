About Us

My Mother Charitable Trust

Our trust is dedicated to supporting individuals affected by cancer, AIDS, and severe viral infections by providing medical assistance, physical development, and essential care. We aim to spread awareness and offer specialized treatments for those in critical stages of these illnesses. Additionally, we promote micro-finance initiatives, SHGs, and MSMEs, and work on publications and media outreach related to health issues.

Our trust also focuses on establishing and running educational institutions, offering technical, medical, and vocational training, including specialized programs for underprivileged and handicapped individuals, fostering holistic development and inclusive education.

Blood Donation Camp

A blood donation camp was recently held at Pulivalam Rajalakshmi marriage hall near Tiruvarur, inaugurated by Deputy Superintendent of Police Manikandan. The camp was a joint effort by My Mother Charitable Trust, Anbagam Charitable Trust, and Tiruvarur Government Medical College. The event saw a great response, with 50 individuals donating blood to support the noble cause. Notable attendees included Varadarajan, founder of Anbagam Charitable Trust, Venkatesh Kumar, executive director of My Mother Charitable Trust, and Muruganantham, executive officer of ONGC, who all participated in the programme.

Awareness Programmes

Cancer Awareness

At Tiruvarur New Bharat Matric Higher Secondary School, the My Mother Charity Rotary Club Kings and Vijayapuram Rotary Club organized a distribution of cancer awareness leaflets. Additionally, a complimentary hearing aid testing and fitting program for children was conducted under the guidance of DMK City Secretary Prakash, with the founder of My Mother Charity, Kalachelvi, presiding over the event at Rotary Club Kings. President Karunanidhi extended a warm welcome to the attendees, and the program saw participation from over 100 individuals.

Cancer Awareness

At Thiruvarur Bus Station, My Mother Charity and Rotary Club Kings organized a cancer awareness pamphlet distribution program under the leadership of City Police Inspector Ramesh. Police Inspector Ramesh explained to the general public about the effects of cancer and gave leaflets to the public.These programs were presided over by City Police Sub-Inspector Thiagarajan, founder of My Mother Charity K Kalachelvi. Many people participated.

Eye Treatment Camp And Sapling Distribution

At Kitaramkondan Thiagarajah School in Tiruvarur Urban, free eye treatment camp and saplings were distributed to the public on environmental awareness followed by pamphlets on cancer. The program was presided over by K Kalachelvi, Founder of My Mother Charity, in which many people including Rotary Club Kings President Karunanidhi City Councilor Purushothaman and social activist Ramesh participated.

"Thiruvika Government College of Arts, Thiruvarur, hosted a Blood Typing Camp and Cancer Awareness Program. Cancer prevention leaflets were distributed to thousands of students. Distinguished guests included Rajavel (My Mother Charity), Venkatesh Kumar (Rotary Club Kings), and Dr. Geetha (Karunanidhi College Principal)."
